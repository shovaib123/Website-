Adhyay 4: Prakash ka Paravartan

Jab prakash kisi chikni satah, jaise darpan, se takra kar wapas laut ata hai, to is prakriya ko prakash ka paravartan kehte hain. Paravartan ke do mukhya niyam hote hain — pehla, aapatan kon paravartan kon ke barabar hota hai, aur dusra, aapatit kiran, paravartit kiran aur abhilamb teeno ek hi samtal mein hote hain...

Samtal darpan mein bana pratibimb aabhasi, sidha aur darpan ke peeche utni hi doori par banta hai jitni vastu darpan ke aage hoti hai...
